Olivia's Asteroid's Demo

Press WASD to move the spaceship
IF YOU INCREASE/DECREASE SPEED, YOU WON'T SEE A DIFFERENCE UNTIL YOU PRESS ONE OF THE WASD KEYS (MOVEMENT KEYS) AGAIN.
Press E to increase speed to a max of 10.
Press Q to decrease speed to a minimum of 1.
Press R to enter hyperspace, stopping the spaceship's movement, making the pointer face a random direction, and placing it at a random location.
